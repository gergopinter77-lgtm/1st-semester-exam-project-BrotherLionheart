    • The Brothers Lionheart

    The epic tale follows two loving brothers, Karl
    (Rusky) and Jonatan, who reunite in the afterlife
    paradise of Nangijala. They discover a
    neighboring valley ruled by the tyrant Tengil and
    courageously dedicate themselves to
    overthrowing him and fighting for the
    oppressed's freedom.

    Astrid Lindgren

    Astrid Lindgren (19072002) was the
    celebrated Swedish author who created the
    iconic Pippi Longstocking in 1945 and penned
    over 30 books, such as The Brothers Lionheart.
    She was also an activist, using her fame to
    champion children's rights and animal welfare.

    Ilon Wikland

    Illon Wikland is a renowned Estonian-born
    Swedish artist and illustrator, best known for
    bringing to life the beloved characters in the
    majority of Astrid Lindgren's books, Including
    the brothers Lionheart. Born in Estonia in 1930,
    she arrived in Sweden as a refugee in 1944.
