    • Skarntyden is an amateur theater group known
    for its creative spirit and strong sense of
    community. The group brings together
    performers, volunteers, and theater enthusiasts of
    all ages who share a passion for storytelling and
    stage craft. Skarntyden typically produces a mix of
    classic plays, original scripts, and light-hearted
    performances, emphasizing collaboration,
    inclusivity, and learning by doing. Whether on
    stage or behind the scenes, members work
    together to create enjoyable productions that
    celebrate local talent and the joy of theater.
